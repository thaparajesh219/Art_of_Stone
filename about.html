The Artist's Journey

From Passion to Profession

A Legacy in Stone

The story of our studio begins in the heart of Nepal, where a childhood fascination with the shapes and textures of river stones grew into a lifelong passion. What started as a simple hobby of carving small figures for family and friends has blossomed into a professional craft, honed over 25 years of dedicated practice.

Every chisel mark is a testament to this journey, blending traditional Nepali techniques with a personal touch that captures profound emotion.

More Than Art, a Memory in Stone

Each creation is an intimate dialogue between the artist and the stone. For memorial sculptures, this connection runs deeper. We understand that these are not just statues; they are tributes to loved ones, tangible memories carved with empathy and respect. The goal is to create a piece that not only honors a life but also brings a sense of peace and remembrance to those left behind.
